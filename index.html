Lion

Lions are known for their iconic roar, which can be heard up to 8 kilometers away.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo.
  • Average Length: 3 meters.
  • Average Lifespan: 10 to 14 years.
  • Habitat: Savannas, grasslands, dense bush, and forests.

Lions (Panthera leo) inhabit savannas, grasslands, thick bush, and forests. They have golden-yellow fur, with males sporting manes that vary from blonde to black. Females lack manes. Social by nature, lions live in prides and are skilled hunters with excellent eyesight. Their distinctive roar communicates with other lions and marks territory. In the wild, lions live 10 to 14 years, but in captivity, they can live up to 20 years.
